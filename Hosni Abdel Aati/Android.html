Android Programming: The Big Nerd Ranch Guide

Author: Bill Phillips

Size: 6.4MB

Rating: ★★★★★

About the Book

Bill Phillips is an author, programmer, and Android app development expert. He works with Big Nerd Ranch, a company that offers popular courses in game and app development. He wrote "Android Programming: The Big Nerd Ranch Guide" with Brian Hardy, which provides hands-on learning with practical examples and clear explanations of core concepts and APIs.

Details

Language: English

Genre: programmer, Android

Publication Date: April 10, 2015

ISBN: 978-1111273565

Quotes

" The book is suitable for programmers working on video games and beginners who want to learn good programming patterns to improve their performance. "

Summary

The book offers a comprehensive coverage of popular patterns in Android app development, including the use of Kotlin and the latest best practices in Android. It includes practical examples and exercises to help readers apply what they learn. With its hands-on design and clear visual aids, the book enhances understanding of the patterns. Additionally, it is available in various formats, including both print and digital editions.
